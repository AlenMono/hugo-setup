How It Works

—

Dokko uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for precise, contextually relevant answers:

Answer Generation

Uses this data to craft detailed, accurate responses, achieving a level of personalization that standard models can't match.

Information Retrieval

Quickly finds the most relevant data for user queries using state-of-the-art hybrid techniques

Dokko supports multiple Large Language Models (LLMs), allowing you to choose the best AI engine for your specific needs and customize responses to reflect your organization's unique characteristics. For even greater degree of personalization, we help with fine-tuning and model customization.
